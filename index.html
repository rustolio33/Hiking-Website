Hiking Safety

While spending time in the outdoors is fun and healthy, it also comes with risks. However, with some precautions, you can have a fun, healthy and safe time.

  • Watch out for wildlife
  • Let someone know where you are going and how long you will be gone
  • Carry a fully charged cell phone
  • Carry a physical map if you are in an unfamiliar area
  • Carry plenty of water and snacks
  • While rare, Pennsylvania is home to two different species of poisonious snakes: The copper head, and the Eastern Diamondback Rattlesnake
